Our scores range from 0 (worst) to 100 (best). The total score represents an average of the individual score for each category.

Safety scores are calculated based on crime data reported for California in 2015.The dataset is filtered for violent crimes (homicide, rape, robbery and assault)reported within 5 km of the user's address. Crime rates are estimated using population density data for the area, compared to the range of crime rates reported for cities in LA and assigned a score based on where the neighborhood's crime rates fall.

Commute time scores are based on estimated travel times provided by Google Directions. Scores range from 0 (travel time is greater than 2 hours) to 100 (travel time is less than 20 min).

Walkability scores are based on the number of stores and restaurants located within a 600 m radius of the user's address. Scores range from 0 (no stores or restaurants) to 100 (greater than 20 stores and/or restaurants)
